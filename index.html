Help

About MyGolfFacts

MyGolfFacts is an app to help you keep track of your golf statistics, so you can improve your game. If you keep track of your stats, over time you'll be able to identify areas where you need to practice more.

How to Use

On the Settings page, you can select which stats you want to track. You can track as many or as little statistics as you like

Select Play a Round, and begin playing. Select "Create New Course" to save the course data as you play. After playing a course once, you can save it, so you don't need to enter the data for the course every time. Possibly in the future the app will be able to download course data so you don't have to enter it in manually.

On the Home screen, select My Stats to view all your stats.
